Skinny by Jessica Elliot

Skinny by Jessica Elliot was born in mid-2008 from a premonition by the sterling silver jewelry designer that the economy and general climate of our country was changing...it started with the prices of precious metal skyrocketing-- and she decided to launch a costume jewelry line where she could do big and bold pieces that were accessible to everyone...made in the USA, this line utilizes Swarovski crystal and hand-enameling, Semi-Precious stones, leather, and made a name for itself specializing in rings, bangles and stud earrings. She has added a great collection of necklaces as well…

After 3 years of success with this line, she launched belts and scarves under the same brand to create a full service accessories/lifestyle shopping experience. The scarves with their original prints and 100% super soft cotton voile have caught the eyes of all the trendsetting celebrities and boutiques, as well as her leather and suede luxury belts at affordable prices.

Stela

Stela 9 was founded in the summer of 2010 by an ex archaeologist turned scavenger hunter. The collection focuses on preserving ancient weaving traditions in Guatemala and Colombia and sourcing local leathers and using native stones. Many of the textiles are are one of a kind and handmade by skilled artisans. A portion of the proceeds directly contributes to the sustainability of the communities that these cherished items travel from.
