Information We Collect

Such information is collected through the use of "cookies," which are text files that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. These cookies are set to your computer when you register or submit online forms.

Some portions of the Site may require you to give us personally-identifiable information such as your name, job title, company, physical mailing address, e-mail address, telephone and fax numbers (collectively, “Personal Information”) and, if applicable, financial information such as your credit card, billing or other payment information (collectively, “Financial Information”), to, among other things: (i) register for attendance at certain ENK trade shows, conferences and events; (ii) obtain access to certain online content and functionality; and (iii) subscribe to our publications. How We Use Your Information
If you elect to provide us with Personal Information, we use it primarily to deliver the service you requested. We also may use Personal Information to contact you about sales, special offers and new Site features, unless you have opted not to receive promotional communications.

If you submit Financial Information, we use that information primarily to verify your credit and collect payments for your purchases, orders, registrations etc.

The non-personally identifiable information we collect through the use of cookies is used to improve this Site and to customize your browsing experience when you visit the Site by providing your browser with information tailored to your preferences and needs. For example, cookies allow us to: (i) identify your computer and automatically access your account information, (ii) facilitate your navigation of the Site, (iii) measure Site usage to specific areas (and this data may be aggregated with usage data from all Site visitors), and (iv) maintain information about your use of this Site. Most browsers accept cookies automatically but allow you to disable them. We recommend that you leave the cookies enabled so that you can receive a more personalized browsing experience when visiting this Site.
